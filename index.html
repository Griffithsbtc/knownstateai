The KnownState Method: Practical AI Implementation

1. ASSESS

We assess how AI is actually being used in your business today. What tools are your teams using? ChatGPT? Microsoft Copilot? Custom prompts? We map your current state.

2. IDENTIFY

We identify what's working, what's assumed, and what's broken. Not all AI usage delivers value, we find your wins and fix the gaps. Clear ROI tracking starts here.

3. ALIGN

We align AI usage to your actual business roles and responsibilities. Sales teams need different AI tools than admin. We match AI to real work, not generic training.

4. IMPROVE

We improve continuously, not once, not randomly. Track time saved, quality improvements, and business impact. Refine prompts, scale what works, cut what doesn't.

Our philosophy is simple: if it's not known, we don't pretend it is. That's why every engagement starts by establishing your knownstate, your actual baseline for AI usage and outcomes.
