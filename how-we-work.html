How We Work

Three steps. A clear outcome at every stage.

Step 1

20-Minute Conversation

We start with a short conversation about where AI sits in your business right now. What tools are your teams using? What's working? What isn't?

What we cover

→ Which AI tools your team is currently using (ChatGPT, Copilot, custom tools)
→ Where AI is saving time and where it's creating frustration
→ Your goals for efficiency, quality, consistency, or all three
→ Whether a deeper engagement makes sense
Step 2

AI Discovery Session

We look at the actual workflows, prompts, and outputs happening across roles. Not theoretical use cases. Real ones.

We map what's being used, how it's being used, and where the gaps are. This gives you the data to build something useful.

What's included

→ Hands-on review of current AI usage across your team
→ Role-by-role assessment, because different roles need different AI approaches
→ Prompt audit, reviewing what your team is actually asking AI to do
→ Quick wins identified on the spot, things we can improve immediately
→ Gap analysis covering what's missing, what's broken, what's assumed
Step 3

The KnownState

Your deliverable. A clear, actionable report that shows exactly where your business stands with AI, not where you think it stands. This becomes your baseline for every decision going forward.

Your deliverable includes

→ AI maturity score by team and role
→ Current vs recommended AI usage mapped per workflow
→ Specific prompt improvements with before/after examples
→ Priority recommendations ranked by impact and effort
→ Measurable baselines so you can track improvement over time

What happens after the assessment?

The KnownState report is yours. Most businesses want help acting on it. Here's what that can look like:

AI Training

Tailored workshops for your team, built from your actual workflows, not generic AI overviews.

Prompt Development

Custom prompts and templates designed for your specific roles, tools, and business context.

Ongoing Support

Monthly check-ins to track progress, refine what's working, and scale what delivers results.
